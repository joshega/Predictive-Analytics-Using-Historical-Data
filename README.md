# Predictive Analytics Using Historical Data

This Power BI dashboard analyzes historical sales data to identify trends, monitor business performance, and forecast future sales. The dashboard includes key performance indicators such as Total Sales, Total Profit, and Total Customers, along with interactive visualizations for monthly sales trends, profit growth, marketing spend versus sales analysis, and seasonal sales distribution.

A forecasting model is applied to historical sales data to predict future sales performance and visualize forecast uncertainty through confidence intervals. Interactive filters allow users to explore data across different dates and seasons, enabling deeper analysis and data-driven decision-making.

### Key Features

* Historical Sales Trend Analysis
* Sales Forecasting with Confidence Intervals
* Monthly Sales Performance Tracking
* Marketing Spend vs Sales Correlation Analysis
* Profit Growth Trend Monitoring
* Seasonal Sales Distribution Analysis
* Interactive Date and Season Filters

### Tools Used

* Power BI Desktop
* DAX Measures
* Forecasting Analytics
* Data Visualization Techniques

### Outcome

The dashboard helps businesses understand past performance, identify growth patterns, evaluate the impact of marketing activities, and make informed decisions based on future sales predictions.
